Administrative tasks:

  • I am a member of the Organizing Committee of the Conference on Lie Theory and Related Topics that took place 03-07 February 2025 at New York University Abu Dhabi.
  • Since September 2024, I am a member of the organization committee of the Post-Doc seminar at New York University Abu Dhabi.
  • I was a member of the Organizing Committee of the second edition of the MAThEOR Days that took place 10-12 July 2024.
  • I was a member of the Organizing Committee of the Conference on Algebra and Deformations that took place 18-21 June 2024 in Mulhouse.
  • In March 2024, I was a member of the Organizing Committee of the Math Day 2024 that took place on March 2nd at New York University Abu Dhabi.
  • In June 2023, I organized together with Victoria Callet-Feltz the first edition of the MAThEOR Days, a scientific meeting between PhD students and young researchers from Alsace and the surrounding areas.
  • In June 2022, I was a member of the Organizing Committee of the Conference of differential geometry, contact geometry, dynamical systems and beyond, in memory of Robert Lutz that took place in Mulhouse, France.
  • From September 2021 to September 2023, I was elected representative for the doctoral students at the board of the Doctoral School ED 269. I took part in councils in Mulhouse and in Strasbourg.
